What is it?

The Midnight Runner is a fun project of font pairing using Google Font content. It can be useful for anyone, Designer like Developer or other.

Value for you: find inspiration, create your own font combinations and discover the unknown fonts of Google Font.

This platform is built for laptop with only Javascript, HTML5 and CSS3. Webfont Loader is used to load font-face.

Shortcuts

  • or R to refresh fonts
  • Click on text to prevent refresh
  • S to reverse font title and body text
  • Space to open your pockets
  • 1 until 5 to save in your pocket
  • Esc to exit

The Midnight Runner is a font pairing generator project by Centis Menant. Powered by Google Fonts, Webfont Loader & love.
